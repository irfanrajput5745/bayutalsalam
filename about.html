About Us

Bayut alsalam properties is brokerage firm in Dubai with integrity and commitment to their business. We take pride in our specialized sales of Ready and off plan properties while taking care of our clients from buying and purchasing till they get their investor visas through Dubai land department. We have specialized in creating patterns to leave footprints for assets of tomorrow ensuring the best consultancy. We are studying market trends for investors to understand how each trend may benefit investors to generate Returns on their equity and investment.


Our team bring over a decade of experience in Dubai real estate industry on the table to provide solutions and not just close a deal. Whether you are looking to buy or sale your property team Bayut Alsalam is always willing to take the lead to analyze the potential assets. The industrial knowledge and experience, enables us to send listings across not just clients but also top brokers of Dubai. We are committed to deliver you results. We are partners with P.R.O companies, travel and tourism companies as well mortgage brokerage companies to assist you at our best in every field. We understand buying house is not just an investment it’s possibly your home and we want to bring you comfort throughout the process of buying till moving in as you may.


Team Bayut Alsalam provides parallel solutions and services to all your needs and delivers results beyond your expectations.

Our Mission

A company providing real estate brokerage services in order to make your investment an Asset. We are providing consultancy, finances to managing your asset, complete in-house 360 services through our professional, informative, experienced and devoted team at Bayut Alsalam properties.
